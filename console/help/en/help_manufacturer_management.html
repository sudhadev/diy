Add New Manufacturer
  • Move the mouse pointer into the "Manufacturers" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window.
  • Click on "Add Manufacturers". "Add New Manufacturer" window will appear. Fill the following required field and click on the "Add" button.
    1. Manufacturer Name
 Manufacturer List
  • Move the mouse pointer into the "Manufacturers" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window.
  • Click on "Manufacturer List" tab.  Following columns are available in the Manufacturer List.
    1. Row Number
    2. Names of the Manufacturers
    3. Email Addresses of the Users and Suppliers who added Manufacturers ("Added By" field)
    4. Added Date
    5. Edit and Delete icons

    By clicking on the , can edit Manufacturer Name. After edit data, click on the "Edit" button.

    There is another section called "Merge Manufacturer". If a Supplier has added an invalid Manufacturer (e.g.: Larfage) to the system, and also there already exist the valid manufacturer (e.g.: Lafarge) in the system, then you can't change it to Lafarge by using Edit Manufacture option, because the similar manufacture is already exist within the system.

    In this time you can use Merge Manufacturer option to merge all the data in the wrong Manufacturer to the correct one. Once you select the correct manufacturer from the given drop down list and clicks on the button, the system will reconfirm it with you. Once you approve it, all the listings available within the wrong manufacturer will be moved to the correct Manufacturer and then system will remove the wrong Manufacturer from the system.

    Once this is done, it's impossible to recall it. Administrator needs to be very carefully when he use this option.

    By clicking on the , can delete Manufacturers. Once you click on that icon, you will be prompted to confirm the deletion.

Click on the "OK" button to confirm the deletion. If there are existing listings for the selected manufacturer, you cannot delete.
