Order List
  • Move the mouse pointer into the "Revenue" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window.
  • Click on "Order List" tab.  Following details available in the customers list.
    1. Total Orders and Total Purchases in GBP(Yellow Strip on top of the Order List)
    2. Row Number
    3. Invoice Number
    4. Amount in GBP
    5. Customer Name
    6. Customer Email Address
    7. Date and Time of the transaction
  • If lot of orders is placed in the system, the pagination numbers will appear on top of the Order List and by click on those admin can browse through all the orders.
  • By clicking on Invoice Number for a particular transaction, you can view the invoice for that transaction. Following details are available in the Invoice.
    1. Invoice No
    2. Date of the Transaction
    3. Time of the Transaction
    4. Description / Details of the Transaction
    5. Sub Total of the Transaction
    6. VAT amount of the Transaction
    7. Total of the Transaction
    8. Customer First Name
    9. Customer Last Name
    10. Customer Email Address
    11. Customer Company
    12. Customer Address, Street, City, Postal Code and Country
    13. Customer Telephone number
    14. Customer Fax Number
    15. Customer Mobile Number

      16. When Clicked on the it will popup the Invoice and admin can take out a print out of it using print dialog box.

  • Admin can search for particular Invoice number or an e-mail address inside the order list by typing it on the text field. Then select "Invoice" or "E-mail" from the dropdown and click Search.
  • Admin can Filter the results in Order List according to the time of the transaction (duration) and subscription type. Selecting "Current Date" will show the transactions for Today. Selecting "Current Month" will show the transactions for this Month. Selecting "Current Year" will show the transactions for this Year. Selecting "Duration" will show the transactions for all time. Selecting "Suppliess" will show the transactions done under Supplies. Selecting "Services" will show the transactions done under Services. Selecting "Classified Ads" will show the transactions done under Classified Ads. Selecting "Subscription" will show the transactions done under all three catagories.
  • Admin can Sort the Order List by date of the transaction by selecting "Date" in the Sort By dropdown. Or he can sort Order List by name of the customer by selecting "Name" in the Sort By dropdown

    • By clicking on the , you can delete Orders. Once you click on that icon, you will be prompted to confirm the deletion. Click on the "OK" button to confirm the deletion or click "Cancel" to cancel.
