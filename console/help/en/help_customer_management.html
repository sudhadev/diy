Customer List
  • Move the mouse pointer into the "Customers" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window.
  • Click on "Customer List" tab.
  • Following details available in the customers list.
    1. Row Number
    2. Customer First Name
    3. Customer Last Name
    4. Customer E-Mail Address
    5. Customer Telephone number
    6. Date Customer registered with the System

    By clicking on the , you can view more details of the particular customer. Following details are available in more details section.

    1. Customer First Name
    2. Customer Last Name
    3. Customer E-Mail Address
    4. Customer Company
    5. Customer Address, Street, City, Postal Code and Country
    6. Customer Telephone number
    7. Customer Fax Number
    8. Customer Mobile Number
    9. Customer Subscriptions
    10. Customer Package

    By clicking on the , you can view Order Details for a particular customer. For more information about Order Details refer Revenue->Order List section in the help.

    By clicking on the , you can delete customers. Once you click on that icon, you will be prompted to confirm the deletion. Click on the "OK" button to confirm the deletion or click "Cancel" to cancel.

  • You can filter the Customer List as Suppliers and Buyers using the "Filter By" section. Click on the first drop-down in the right side of "Filter By" and Select "Suppliers" or "Buyers".
  • And then can filter Buyers or Suppliers According to their status in the System as;
    1. Active
    2. Approved
    3. Inactive
    4. Deleted
    5. Blocked
    6. Waiting
    7. Rejected

      8. Click on the second drop-down in the right side of "Filter By" and Select "Active", "Approved", "Inactive", "Deleted" , "Blocked", "Waiting" or "Rejected".

  • You can sort Customer List by Customer Name or Customer Email Address using the "Order By" section. Click on the drop-down in the right side of "Order By" and Select "Name" or "Email"
  • You can search in Customer List by Customer Name or Customer Email Address. Enter particular word you want to search in the Customer List. Select "Name" from drop-down if you want to search by Customer Name. Select "E-Mail" from drop-down if you want to search by Customer Email Address.
 Pending Approval
  • Move the mouse pointer into the "Customers" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window. Click on "Pending Approval" tab.
  • Or move the mouse pointer into the "Customers" tab in the menu bar in Admin Console Window. Click on "Customer List" tab. Select "Suppliers" and "Waiting" from the Filter By Section.
  • Ones you access this section, it will display a list of customers who are waiting for admin approval. Click on the .
  • Then the system will show the information relevant to the customer and admin can "Approve" or "Reject" the customer using the dropdown in the top part. Just select "Approve" or "Reject" and click submit.
